Tokimon


    A Tokimon is a rare creature found in the remote areas of Korea. For the most part, they resemble rabbits and the only form of communication they have with us is the ability to say their own name. Tokimons come in all shapes and sizes, have special abilities, and tend to fight with each other on occasion. Each Tokimon could have the ability to fly, fight, throw fire, spray water, electrify, and freeze other Tokimons; their ability is measured by an integer between 0 and 100.
